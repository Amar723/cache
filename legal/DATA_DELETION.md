# Cache — Data Deletion Instructions

**App name:** Cache
**Contact:** goldenavenuesoftware@gmail.com
**Last updated:** 03/07/2026

Cache lets you save places from videos to a personal map. This page explains how
to delete your data from Cache.

## What data Cache stores

When you use Cache, we store:

- Your account (email address and username)
- Your saved pins ("stashes"): place name, coordinates, category, notes, the
  video URL you shared, and its thumbnail URL
- Your friend connections
- Any photos you uploaded

Cache does **not** store your Instagram or TikTok account credentials, and does
not have access to your Instagram or TikTok account. Video thumbnails are
fetched from public preview endpoints using only the link you shared.

## Option 1 — Delete your account in the app (immediate)

1. Open **Cache** on your device.
2. Go to the **Profile** tab.
3. Tap **Delete account** and confirm.

This immediately and permanently deletes your account and **all** associated
data: every saved pin, your friend connections, your profile, and your login.
This cannot be undone.

## Option 2 — Request deletion by email

If you can no longer access the app, email **goldenavenuesoftware@gmail.com** from the email
address associated with your Cache account with the subject line
"**Data deletion request**". We will delete your account and all associated
data within 30 days and confirm by reply.
