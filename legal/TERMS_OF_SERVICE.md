## 1. What Cache does

Cache is a location-saving app. When you see a TikTok, Instagram Reel, or other
post about a place worth visiting, you can share it to Cache, tag the place, and
Cache drops a pin on your personal map. When you are near a saved place, Cache
can send you a local notification; when you arrive, you can mark the place as
visited. You can also add friends and view pins they have chosen to share with
you, subject to per-pin privacy settings.
